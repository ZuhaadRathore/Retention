About Retention

Retention is a modern flashcard application that combines the proven effectiveness of spaced repetition with the power of artificial intelligence. Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a professional learning new skills, or a lifelong learner exploring new topics, Retention adapts to your learning style.

Built with cutting-edge technology including React, TypeScript, and Tauri, Retention delivers a native desktop experience that's fast, reliable, and works completely offline. Your data stays on your device, giving you full control and privacy.

The AI-powered grading system provides intelligent feedback on your answers, helping you understand not just what you got wrong, but why. This accelerates learning and helps build deeper understanding of complex topics.
